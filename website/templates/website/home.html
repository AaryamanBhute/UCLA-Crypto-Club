Why we're here!

From a week-1 conversation about the GME and AMC short squeeze outside of bcafe, we noticed that there were many finance clubs but they all missed an increasingly prevalent aspect of finance: Crypto.

We are also looking to overcome a challenge that comes along with clubs in general: accessibility. Most clubs require everyone to meet at the same time at a place on campus. What happens if you aren't on campus? What happens if you have a class at the meeting time? Another meeting? Thats part of the inspiration for UCLA Crypto. A club available 24/7.

Who doesn't like a bit of friendly competition? In addition to being able to practice trading crypto with dummy money, you can see who's portfolio is the largest on the leaderboard! (You can choose to stay anonymous if you prefer)
