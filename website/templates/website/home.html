— Welcome to UCLA Crypto! —

A Club on Your Time!

As UCLA students, we're always busy balancing our extracurriculars and classes.

Unfortunately, sometimes an in person meeting is just not possible that day or at that time. Although we still have some in person meetings, the core of the club is on your screen right now!

We are currently in the middle of a financial transformation with the emergence of cryptocurruncies and we were shocked to find there was no club at UCLA for crypto education. That's why we're here!

Our current mission is to get students comfortable investing in cryptocurruncies and provide some friendly competition along the way.

If you have any suggestions or questions please feel free to reach out through the contacts below!

If you're new, click the start tab to learn more about how you can get involved!
