— How To Get Started! —

First, login with your "@g.ucla.edu" emails, you can't be on the leaderboard from any non-ucla email but you can still have a portfolio. This is to prevent people from using multiple accounts which would ruin the leaderboard competition.

The best way to get started is with the portfolio page, explore the UI and start buying and selling cryptocurruncies

After getting a handle on your portfolio, the next step is to take a look at the leaderboards

The leaderboard page updates every hour and contains the leaderboard for all users as well as analytics regarding your portfolio.

The trading simulation give you starting funds based on how far into the current "season" you create your account, Each week you will recieve an additional $10,000 to your portfolio. This system is setup such that regardless of when you start trading or login to the site, everyone will have the same initial cash.

This site is NEW and likely isn't perfect, if there is anything that is not working correctly or if you have any suggestions and improvements, please email uclacryptoclub@gmail.com
