— About UCLA Crypto! —

In 2009 Bitcoin was released as the first modern cryptocurrency, a year later in 2010 2 young men bought a pizza for 10,000 bitcoin worth just 41$ then but worth 680,000,000$ at the highest price of bitcoin.

We can talk about how it was a horrible purchase in hindsight but how were they supposed to know how big crypto has gotten in recent years?

In just a few decades, people have been able to revolutionize their financial situation with the emergence of cryptocurruncies.

Despite the now main stream appeal of crypto, there still wasn't a presence of crypto in the UCLA club sphere. That's why we're here.

We're looking to fill a void and revolutionize what a club is at the same time, it doesn't need to be a time-commitment, drop by the site at any time to play the simulation and see how you stack up against the rest of UCLA while learning more about crypto.
